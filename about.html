About EduQuiz

EduQuiz is an innovative digital learning platform created to make education exciting, interactive, and accessible to all learners. The platform transforms traditional learning into an engaging experience where students can test their knowledge, track their progress, and enjoy healthy academic competition. Through interactive quizzes, instant feedback, and user-friendly design, EduQuiz motivates learners to study actively and helps teachers assess understanding in real time.

Whether used in classrooms or for self-paced study, EduQuiz bridges the gap between learning and fun. It encourages consistent revision, promotes collaboration, and builds confidence by allowing learners to learn from mistakes instantly. With features designed for Ghanaian learners and beyond, EduQuiz supports the goal of making digital education more inclusive and impactful.

Our Mission

Our mission is to empower students with engaging and meaningful digital learning experiences that spark curiosity, enhance critical thinking, and inspire lifelong learning. EduQuiz aims to make knowledge acquisition enjoyable by combining technology, creativity, and education to improve academic performance and learner participation.

Our Vision

To become Ghana’s leading and most trusted digital quiz platform that transforms how students learn, teachers assess, and schools integrate technology into education. We envision a future where every learner—regardless of location or background—has access to fun, quality, and personalized learning opportunities powered by EduQuiz.

Meet the Developer

Alex Mawunya Tagbor is a passionate educator, web developer, and advocate for technology-driven education. He holds a Bachelor of Education (B.Ed) degree with a specialization in JHS Mathematics and ICT, and is currently pursuing a Master of Philosophy (MPhil) in Information Technology at the Akenten Appiah-Menka University of Skills Training and Entrepreneurial Development (AAMUSTED).

Alex’s deep interest in using technology to enhance teaching and learning inspired the creation of EduQuiz. With a blend of classroom experience and software development skills, he aims to make digital learning more inclusive, engaging, and effective for students across Ghana and beyond. His vision is to bridge the gap between education and innovation, equipping learners with 21st-century skills to thrive in a technology-driven world.
